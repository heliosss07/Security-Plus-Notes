## 1.1 Security Controls

- **Technical Controls (Logical Controls):**
    
    These use technology to enforce security. They are implemented through hardware or software to protect systems and data.
    
- **Managerial Controls (Administrative Controls):**
    
    These are policies, procedures, and guidelines established by management to define the organization’s security strategy and risk management framework.
    
- **Operational Controls:**
    
    These are day-to-day processes and actions carried out by people (not automated systems) to enforce and maintain security.
    
- **Physical Controls:**
    
    These protect the physical environment where systems and data are located. They prevent unauthorized physical access, damage, or interference.
